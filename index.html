Fragrant Radish Veggie Soup

Ingredients:

  • Radishes (sliced)
  • Carrots (sliced)
  • Onion (chopped)
  • Garlic (minced)
  • Ginger (grated)
  • Vegetable broth
  • Soy sauce
  • Salt and pepper
  • Green onions (chopped, for garnish)

Instructions:

  1. In a large pot, heat some oil over medium heat. Add the chopped onion, minced garlic, and grated ginger. Sauté until fragrant.
  2. Add the sliced radishes and carrots to the pot. Sauté for a few minutes until slightly softened.
  3. Pour in the vegetable broth and bring to a boil. Reduce the heat and let it simmer for about 15-20 minutes, until the vegetables are tender.
  4. Season with soy sauce, salt, and pepper to taste. Stir well to combine.
  5. Serve the Fragrant Radish Veggie Soup hot, garnished with chopped green onions.

Enjoy the comforting and delicious flavors of Fragrant Radish Veggie Soup!
